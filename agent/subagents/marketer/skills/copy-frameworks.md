Use when drafting marketing copy, ad text, social posts, email subjects, or campaign messaging.

## Frameworks

### AIDA
- **Attention** — hook with a specific pain or outcome
- **Interest** — one concrete benefit or proof point
- **Desire** — show who it is for and why now
- **Action** — one clear CTA

### PAS
- **Problem** — name the frustration plainly
- **Agitate** — show the cost of staying stuck
- **Solution** — introduce the offer as the relief

## Social post checklist
- Lead with the hook in the first line
- One idea per post
- End with a single CTA
- Match tone to audience (professional, playful, urgent, etc.)

## Platform notes
- **X** — short, self-contained, under 280 characters
- **LinkedIn** — professional tone; context before pitch
- **Instagram** — conversational; hashtags optional and sparse
- **Facebook** — friendly and direct
- **Email** — subject line first; body scannable with bullets
